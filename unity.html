ZOMBIE APOCALYPSE

A zombie survival where you have to reach the final stage to escape. To reach it you have to kill as many zombies as possible to get point and buy weapons, skills and unlock areas. Zombies can also drop perks (invisibility, minigun, invincibility and a friendly zombie). they can also drop ammo and things to heal yourself. This game was created by a team of 4 programmers
